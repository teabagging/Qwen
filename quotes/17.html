❓Design an intricate blueprint for a serverless computing architecture using your chosen scripting language, and integrate it with a real-time monitoring system to enhance troubleshooting capabilities, oversee operational efficiency, and reduce downtime. This task requires a multi-tiered critical thinking approach, including the construction of the architecture, the selection of the scripting language, and the incorporation of the monitoring system. Additionally, contemplate the possible elasticity and data protection consequences of your design selections, and justify your choices.

Need to choose a monitoring solution and explain how it's integrated. CloudWatch (AWS), Azure Monitor, Google Cloud Monitoring are natural choices for their respective cloud platforms. For a more general approach, Prometheus/Grafana could be considered, but cloud-native solutions are likely more straightforward for serverless. * **Troubleshooting, Operational Efficiency, Downtime Reduction:** These are the *goals* of the monitoring system. The design should explicitly address these. * **Elasticity and Data Protection:** These are crucial considerations. The design needs to demonstrate awareness of these aspects and how serverless and the chosen components address them. * **Justification:** Every choice needs a rationale. Why Python? Why CloudWatch? It also naturally lends itself to serverless functions. * **Break Down the Use Case into Serverless Functions:** * **Image Upload:** API Gateway + Lambda (handles HTTP requests, uploads to S3). * **Image Processing:** Lambda (triggered by S3 events, performs processing). * **Metadata Storage:** DynamoDB (NoSQL database, stores metadata). * **Result Delivery:** API Gateway + Lambda (retrieves results, serves to users). * **Consider Asynchronous Processing:** Serverless is well-suited for asynchronous tasks.
