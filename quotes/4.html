This empowers the user and directs them to the best source of personalized advice. This makes the user an active participant in finding the right solution. "Comunica claramente tu preocupación." * **Positive and Reassuring Closing:** End on an encouraging note, reiterating that they *can* find glasses they'll feel good about. "Encontrarás unas gafas que te permitan ver bien y sentirte cómodo/a con tu aspecto."7. **Language and Tone:** * **Empathetic and Understanding:** The tone should be reassuring and empathetic throughout. Keep the focus tight on lens thickness and shape. Frame choice and lens type are more directly relevant.* **Reviewing Draft:** Read through the answer as if I were the user.
