❓The average weight of 8 persons increases by a certain amount when a new person comes in place of one of them weighing 45 kg. The weight of the new person is 93 kg. By how many kg does the average weight increase?

💡Let N be the number of persons, which is 8.Let W_1, W_2, ..., W_8 be the weights of the 8 persons.The average weight of these 8 persons is A = frac{W_1 + W_2 + ... + W_8}{8}.The sum of weights is S = W_1 + W_2 + ... + W_8 = 8A.One person weighing 45 kg is replaced by a new person weighing 93 kg.Let's assume without loss of generality that the person with weight W_8 is replaced, and W_8 = 45 kg.The new person has weight 93 kg.The new set of persons has weights W_1, W_2, ..., W_7, W_{new}, where W_{new} = 93 kg.The sum of weights of the new set of persons is S_{new} = W_1 + W_2 + ... + W_7 + W_{new}.We know that W_8 = 45.S = W_1 + W_2 + ... + W_7 + W_8 = W_1 + W_2 + ... + W_7 + 45.S_{new} = W_1 + W_2 + ... + W_7 + 93.We can write S_{new} in terms of S.S_{new} = (W_1 + W_2 + ... + W_7 + 45) - 45 + 93 = S - 45 + 93 = S + (93 - 45) = S + 48.The new average weight is A_{new} = frac{S_{new}}{8} = frac{S + 48}{8} = frac{S}{8} + frac{48}{8} = A + 6.The increase in average weight is A_{new} - A = (A + 6) - A = 6.Alternatively, we can think about the change in the sum of weights.The weight of one person is reduced from 45 kg and increased to 93 kg.The net increase in the sum of weights is 93 - 45 = 48 kg.Since there are 8 persons, the increase in the average weight is the total increase in weight divided by the number of persons.Increase in average weight = frac{text{Increase in sum of weights}}{text{Number of persons}} = frac{48}{8} = 6 kg.Let's use an example to verify.Assume the weight of 8 persons are all 40 kg.Total weight = 8 times 40 = 320 kg.Average weight = 320 / 8 = 40 kg.If one person weighing 45 kg is replaced, this example is not consistent.Let's assume the weights of 7 persons are 40 kg and one person is 45 kg.Weights: 40, 40, 40, 40, 40, 40, 40, 45.Sum of weights = 7 times 40 + 45 = 280 + 45 = 325 kg.Average weight = 325 / 8 = 40.625 kg.Replace the person weighing 45 kg with a person weighing 93 kg.New weights: 40, 40, 40, 40, 40, 40, 40, 93.Sum of new weights = 7 times 40 + 93 = 280 + 93 = 373 kg.New average weight = 373 / 8 = 46.625 kg.Increase in average weight = 46.625 - 40.625 = 6 kg.The increase in average weight is 6 kg.

❓The sum of the coefficients of the even power terms of x in the expansion of (m+x)(1+x)^{4} is 24. Find the value of m.
